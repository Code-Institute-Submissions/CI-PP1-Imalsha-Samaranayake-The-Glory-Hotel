About us

Welcome to the Glory Hotel!!We are one of a leading Standard hotel in Berlin. We have many experience in hospitality and Tourism industry. We are registered as a three star hotel in German Tourist Board. Tourists all arround the world are most welcome to our lovely property. We do have employees who speak English,France,Japanese,Italic,Chinese & Arabic. We have over 100 rooms including Standard,Studio & Suite.We are located in the heart of the Berlin. Near our property there are many more tourist attraction places such as, Brandenburg Gate,Reichstag Building,Charlottenburg Palace,Berlin TV tower,Berlin Wall Memorial ect. Also we do have Aiport Shuttle Service. (Only to Berlin Airport) The distance from the hotel to Berlin City Center is 500m and from the main train station it will take 700m to the Hotel.
